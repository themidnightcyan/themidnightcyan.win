How to Use Pronouns

Quick Reference: She/Her

Subject: "She is working on a new project"

Object: "I saw her at the store"

Possessive: "That's her notebook"

Possessive (standalone): "The essay is hers"

Reflexive: "Athena treated herself to a cup of tea"
